# Digital Confidence Centre — Strategic Analysis
*March 2026 · Two Birds*

## Product Summary

The Digital Confidence Centre (DCC) is a free, self-paced digital literacy programme for Ontario seniors aged 70+. Eleven modules covering iPad/iPhone fundamentals through to AI awareness. Deployed as a static site on GitHub Pages + Cloudflare. Zero operating cost at current scale.

---

## Target Audience

### Primary User: The Senior (70+)
- iPhone/iPad-only household, often gifted by family
- Low digital confidence, high anxiety about making mistakes
- Motivated by staying connected to family and managing health
- Trusts the tool more when introduced by a family member
- Does not self-discover digital literacy tools online

### Primary Acquisition Channel: The Adult Child (40–60)
- Searches for "how to teach my parent to use their iPhone"
- Makes the purchase/referral decision
- Sets up the tool and monitors early progress
- All marketing copy must address this persona first

### Institutional Channel (Deferred)
- Libraries, community centres, long-term care homes, health networks
- Highest revenue ceiling but requires proven user base first
- Trigger: 500+ active users

## Competitive Landscape

| Competitor | Weakness | Our Advantage |
|---|---|---|
| AARP Tech Help | US-focused, generic | Ontario-specific, bilingual |
| Senior Planet | Requires account | No login required |
| YouTube tutorials | No structure | Structured curriculum |
| Library drop-ins | Time-limited, variable quality | Self-paced, always available |
| Local tech support | Paid, intimidating | Free, non-judgmental |

---

## Revenue Model

### Phase 1 — Free (Current)
- No monetisation. Trust building only.
- Cost: $0/month (GitHub Pages + Cloudflare free tier)

### Phase 2 — Individual (500+ users)
- Optional donation / "buy me a coffee"
- Freemium: advanced modules or certificate for small fee

### Phase 3 — B2B White-Label (post-validation)
- Licence the platform to libraries, care homes, health networks
- Monthly SaaS fee per institution
- Highest revenue ceiling, lowest CAC

### Deferred Decision
Non-profit vs. for-profit legal structure deferred until Phase 2 threshold is reached. Cost of structure setup not justified before product-market fit.

---

## Brand Principles

1. **No jargon.** Every explanation in plain language.
2. **No shame.** No error states that make the user feel foolish.
3. **No data commerce.** Trust is the product; selling data destroys it.
4. **Ontario-specific.** CRA, Service Canada, 211, local phone numbers.
5. **Accessibility-first.** WCAG AA minimum, tested at 200% zoom.

---

## Key Risks

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| Seniors never find the tool solo | Adult child acquisition channel |
| Content goes stale | Quarterly review cadence, trust decay system |
| Platform dependency (GitHub) | All content is portable flat files |
| Scope creep | Static-only constraint, no backend rule |
